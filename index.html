Who We Are

DSP Invest is a global trading firm specialising in the import and export of various products. With a strong presence in the international market, the company facilitates trade between businesses in different countries, helping them source high-quality products and expand their reach. The company prides itself on its extensive network of suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors worldwide, enabling it to offer a diverse range of products across different industries. DSP Invest has a 12-year presence in Asia and has been able to nurture a reliable network of trustworthy manufacturers, each carefully selected for their professionalism and the quality of their products.

What We Do

DSP Invest emphasises building strong and long-lasting relationships with both suppliers and customers. By understanding the specific requirements of each client, the company aims to provide tailored solutions and match them with suitable partners to meet their procurement or distribution needs.

Our Promise

DSP Invest stands for reliability, professional competence and an outstanding level of service. For more than 20 years it has been our mission to build long-term sales partnerships in order to sustainably improve the quality of life of our customers.

Our experience in various market sectors and areas makes our company a reliable partner. Since 2018, together with our customers' valued support, we have been able to keep an eye on future global challenges, hence ensuring the certainty of our long term mutual success.
