Who We Are

DSP Invest is an international investment and trading firm committed to connecting global opportunities with local growth. With over a decade of experience in Asia and Europe, we have built a strong and reliable network of trusted suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors across multiple industries. Today, we are proud to bring this expertise to Morocco, with the opening of our new branch in Tinghir. Our mission is to empower local businesses and entrepreneurs by providing access to high-quality products, sustainable energy solutions through our brand Absaarsolar, and tailored investment strategies. By combining our global reach with local insight, DSP Invest is dedicated to driving growth, creating opportunities, and building a brighter, more sustainable future for the Tinghir community and beyond.

What We Do

DSP Invest is dedicated to building strong, long-lasting relationships with both suppliers and clients. By carefully listening to the unique needs of each customer, we provide tailored solutions that connect them with the right partners, ensuring efficient procurement, reliable distribution, and sustainable growth.

Our Promise

At DSP Invest, we stand for trust, professionalism, and excellence in service. For over 20 years, our mission has been to build lasting partnerships that create real value and improve the lives of the people and communities we serve.

With the launch of our branch in Tinghir, Morocco, we renew this promise—bringing global expertise, sustainable solutions through Absaarsolar, and new opportunities that will empower local businesses, families, and the wider community.
